How it works

A service that's automated and trustworthy!

Step 1

Player selects the game they want to play and the stakes they want to play with.

Step 2

Player may invite or wait for someone to join the game, or use a matchmaker to find an opponent.

Step 3

Game finishes, Oracle fetches result (first blood, turret, winner, etc.), smart contract processes reward.

We are building Tronn

A platform that lets eSports players challenge the field and win rewards.
Made possible by smart contracts and oracles in the blockchain.

Game data will be automatically verified,
Reward will be automatically sent.
